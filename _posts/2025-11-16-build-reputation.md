# SOAR
> What is SOAR?

SOAR is a storytelling framework that's clearer and better for documenting growth than STAR.

It has four parts:

**S - Situation**

What happened? What's the context?

**O - Obstacle**

What's the problem? What's the difficulty?

â­ This is the key to SOAR because "Obstacle = where your value appears."

**A - Action**

What did you do? What specific behaviors did you take? (Focus on "I," not the team.)

**R - Result**

What are the results? Numbers, impact, growthâ€”what do people say about you?

# the Three Principles of Marketing

> Put the Three Principles of Marketing in your Elevator Pitch so people remember you, understand you, and want to help you faster.
> 

**AWARENESS - What do you want people to know about you? (Building impressions)**

**VALUE PROPOSITION - Why you? (Your value, your highlights)**

**CALL TO ACTION - What do you want them to do next? (Tell them "what's next")**

**Why do this?**

Because in networking, workplace, or mentor events:

- If you don't give people a label, they won't remember you.
- If you don't talk about your value, they don't know why they should care about you.
- If you don't give next steps, they don't know how to help you.

That's why many people don't make a connection after they introduce themselves.

They're sharing "facts," not "brand."
